## Container editor: move whole ON mask (`editor.html`)

- **Files:** `Sandbox/editor.html` (build **10**)
- **What:** **Move shape: On/Off** toggles drag mode: pointer down on a filled triangle, then drag moves the entire `onKeys` set by the nearest valid lattice translation so every cell stays drawable (`guideKeys`). Pointer delta is mapped through the rotated SVG (`getScreenCTM` on the mesh `g`) to mesh space for stable dragging at any editor rotation. Mutually exclusive with Fill and Eraser; Paint/Eraser/Fill turn move off. One undo restores the pre-drag mask.
- **Why:** User asked to click inside the green cluster and drag it anywhere within the mesh drawable region.

## Container editor: Paint vs Eraser mode (`editor.html`)
