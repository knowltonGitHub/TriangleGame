Container shape editor

Paint one triangle at a time: drag to turn cells ON; click (no drag) toggles. Eraser mode: drag or click turns cells OFF. Move shape mode: click inside the filled area and drag to slide the whole mask (stays inside the drawable region). A subtle blue cross aligned to screen vertical/horizontal through the guide center (two lines) updates when you rotate the editor. Open playground. Save/load JSON (containerCellMask). Fill uses edge neighbors only (undo if it leaks).

Editor orientation: 0 deg CW (step 0 of 6)
