Physics notes (expand)

Lane step: empty downhill same-kind cells, min |cross| to gravity line; tier ties: min |lateral|, then +rightPerp, then along-gravity (removed old interior same-column exclusion so the mesh “below” cell can compete). Opposite-kind flips (wall / interior / sight): same |cross| / along ties prefer maximum +rightPerp. Wall tip: downhill rotation to boundary cell. Interior tip: +rightPerp among interior flips. Wall pivot: downhill flip across a wall edge. Lay-flat: on boundary, more wall contact before lane slide. Crash demo: ?crash=1 or crash-fall-demo.json.
